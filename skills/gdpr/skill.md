# GDPR Skill
Covers all 99 articles of the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679.

Key areas:
- Data subject rights (Art. 12-23)
- Controller and processor obligations (Art. 24-43)
- International data transfers (Art. 44-50)
- Supervisory authorities (Art. 51-76)
- Penalties and enforcement (Art. 77-84)
